### 3. Všelijaké integrály: u-substitúcia

$$
\int_a^b f(x)dx \\
u = u(x) \quad du = u^{\prime}(x)dx \\
x = u^{-1}(x) \quad dx = \frac{1}{u^{\prime}(x)}|_{x = u^{-1}(x)}du \\
u_1 = u(a),\quad u_2 = u(b)
$$



**Príklady**
$$
\int \frac{x}{\sqrt{1+x^2}}dx
$$
